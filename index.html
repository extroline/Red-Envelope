About Red Envelope PDX

Red Envelope Exotic Car Services specializes in high-quality repair, maintenance, and customization of exotic vehicles including Lamborghini, McLaren, Ferrari, Rolls Royce, Porsche, and more. With a passion for precision and excellence, we bring top-tier craftsmanship to Portland's luxury car community.

Our Services

Routine Maintenance

Oil changes, fluid checks, tire rotations, and comprehensive vehicle inspections to keep your car in perfect condition.

Diagnostics & Repairs

Advanced diagnostics with cutting-edge tools and expert repairs on complex exotic car systems.

Performance Upgrades

Engine tuning, suspension enhancements, and other upgrades to elevate your car's performance.

Cosmetic Enhancements

Paint correction, detailing, and custom bodywork to make your vehicle look flawless.

Customization

Personalized interiors, bespoke exteriors, and unique modifications tailored to your style.

Pre-Purchase Inspections

Comprehensive evaluations to help you buy exotic cars with confidence.
